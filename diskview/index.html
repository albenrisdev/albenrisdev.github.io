DiskView

See where your storage goes — and reclaim it. A disk-usage analyzer for macOS.

View on the Mac App Store

DiskView scans the folders you choose and shows what’s taking up space with a proportional treemap and a drill-down hierarchy. It runs in Apple’s App Sandbox, collects no data, and never sends anything off your Mac.

Features

  • Visual treemap of folders and files — click to zoom in.
  • Largest files & folders, ranked by size.
  • Duplicate finder (byte-for-byte) with reclaimable-space totals.
  • Old & unused files, and a cleanup view.
  • Move items to the Trash on request — always recoverable.
