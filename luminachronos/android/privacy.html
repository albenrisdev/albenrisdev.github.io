Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 May 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Lumina Chronos Wear OS application (the “App”) published by Alben Risdev (the “Developer”).

Summary

Lumina Chronos does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. It does not require an account, does not access the internet, and does not include analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.

Data the App handles

  • Timer settings (work / break durations, rounds, volume, vibration, auto-start, tick, ambient display, pause-on-minimize) — stored locally on your watch using Android DataStore. They never leave the device.
  • Active session state (current phase, remaining time, completed rounds) — held in memory and a local snapshot on disk so the timer survives the watch reclaiming the app. Cleared when the session ends or the app is uninstalled.

Permissions the App requests, and why

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — to display the ongoing timer notification on Wear OS 3+ (Android 13+).
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE & FOREGROUND_SERVICE_SPECIAL_USE — to keep the timer visible in ambient (low-power) mode while a session is running, the same pattern Wear OS workout and navigation apps use. The service runs only during an active session and stops immediately on completion, pause, or reset.
  • VIBRATE — for end-of-phase haptic cues.
  • WAKE_LOCK — held only while the countdown is actively ticking, released the moment the session is paused or ends.

What the App does not do

  • No network requests. No servers. The App functions fully offline.
  • No analytics SDKs, no crash-reporting that sends data off-device, no advertising IDs.
  • No access to contacts, calendar, location, microphone, camera, photos, health data, or any sensor beyond the watch’s vibration motor.
  • No cloud backup of timer state. android:allowBackup="false" is set in the manifest.

Children

The App is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly process data from anyone.
