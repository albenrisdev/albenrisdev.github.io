Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 June 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Lumina Chronos iOS application (the “App”) published by Alben Risdev (the “Developer”).

Summary

Lumina Chronos does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. It does not require an account, does not access the internet, and does not include analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.

Data the App handles

  • Timer settings (focus and break durations, rounds per cycle, sound volume, vibration, countdown ticker, auto-start breaks, background animation, pause-on-minimize, orientation) — stored locally on your device using UserDefaults. They never leave the device.
  • Active session state (current phase, remaining time, completed rounds) — held in memory and written to a local snapshot so the timer survives the app being backgrounded or the system reclaiming it. Cleared when the session ends or the app is uninstalled.

Permissions the App requests

The App does not request any iOS runtime permissions. It does not access the network, contacts, calendar, location, microphone, camera, photos, health data, or any sensor beyond the device’s Taptic Engine for haptic cues. Haptics use the standard UIImpactFeedbackGenerator and Core Haptics APIs, which do not require a permission prompt.

What the App does not do

  • No network requests. No servers. The App functions fully offline.
  • No analytics SDKs, no third-party crash-reporting, no advertising identifiers (the App does not use AppTrackingTransparency because it does not track).
  • No iCloud or third-party sync of timer state.
  • No push notifications, no background fetch, no location services.

Children

The App is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly process data from anyone.

Changes to this policy
